JS9 Demo: displaying different FITS data types

  • double 1000x1000 (v=x.y/1000, 8Mb!)

  • int 1000x1000 (v=x*1000+y, 4Mb)
  • short 100x100 (v=x*100+y, small)
  • byte 10x10 (v=x*10+y, tiny)
